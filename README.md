## 📖 Overview

**Israel’s Temple** is a historical ritual simulation game where the player serves as a priest responsible for carrying out sacred duties within the Temple.

Inspired by the rituals described in the Torah, the game challenges players to:

- perform sacrifices
- maintain sacred objects
- assist worshippers bringing offerings
- prepare for festivals
- maintain ritual purity

Every day follows the rhythm of the sacred calendar, including:

- daily sacrifices  
- Sabbath observance  
- monthly New Moon offerings  
- major pilgrimage festivals  

Players begin as a **junior priest** and gradually rise through the ranks of the priesthood.

## 🎮 Gameplay

The core gameplay focuses on **ritual accuracy and temple management**.

### Daily Duties

- Morning sacrifice
- Evening sacrifice
- Maintain altar fire
- Burn incense
- Trim menorah lamps

### NPC Requests

Visitors arrive at the Temple requesting:

- burnt offerings  
- sin offerings  
- guilt offerings  
- thanksgiving offerings  
- purification rituals  

Players must ensure each ritual is performed **correctly and in the proper order**.

---

## 🐑 Sacrifice System

The game implements the sacrificial system described in Leviticus.

| Offering | Purpose |
|--------|--------|
| Burnt Offering | Dedication and atonement |
| Grain Offering | Gift offering of food |
| Peace Offering | Fellowship meal |
| Sin Offering | Atonement for unintentional sin |
| Guilt Offering | Restitution for wrongdoing |
